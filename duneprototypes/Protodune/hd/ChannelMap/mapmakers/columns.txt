The APAname strings follow this convention:

APA_P[nn][N,S][U,L] 

  Where "P" means ProtoDUNE
  nn is the row number (01 or 02)
  N: North, S: South
  U: Upper, L: Lower 

In ProtoDUNE-HD, the North APAs are Lower (inverted), and the South APAs are Upper (upright).
